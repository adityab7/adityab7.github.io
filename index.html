An Independent Alberta

Should Alberta become independent from Canada?



Citizens of Alberta are slowly growing more discontent with the federal government back in Ottawa. They believe that the legislators in Parliament Hill do not have their best interests at heart, and Albertans have numerous reasons to believe this way. The first point always brought up in debates about independence is that Albertan culture widely differs from Canadian culture. Do we really have more in common with a Québécois than an American? This year long inquiry weighs the benefits of staying in the union, and compares it with the reasons to leave.
